Support

How do I log a meal?

Tap the mic button and say what you ate. EasyGains parses it automatically.

How do I delete a meal?

Tap the meal on the home screen to open it, then tap "Delete meal."

How do I cancel Pro?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → EasyGains.

Voice input not working?

Check that microphone and speech recognition permissions are enabled in iPhone Settings → EasyGains.
