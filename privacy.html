Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

EasyGains ("we," "our," or "us") is a voice-first nutrition and fitness tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We do not collect your name, phone number, or payment information.

Profile Data
We store the goals and targets you set within the app: your desired weight, daily calorie target, activity target, and goal type (lose weight, maintain, or build muscle).

Food and Workout Logs
We store the food entries and workout entries you log, including voice transcripts, estimated macros (calories, protein, carbs, fat), meal types, and workout details. This is the core data of the service.

Voice Input
When you use voice logging, your spoken words are transcribed on your device using Apple's Speech Recognition, then sent to our AI service (Anthropic Claude) to parse into structured nutrition or workout data. We store the resulting text transcript alongside your log entry. We do not store raw audio recordings.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide the EasyGains service:

  • To display your nutrition and workout history
  • To calculate your macro targets and progress
  • To enable voice-based food and workout logging via AI parsing

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described below.

Third-Party Services

Supabase — We use Supabase for data storage and user authentication. Your data is stored in Supabase's cloud infrastructure. See their privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

Anthropic — When you log via voice, your transcript is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for parsing. Anthropic processes this data per their usage policies at anthropic.com/privacy. We send only the text of what you said — no account identifiers are included in these requests.

Apple — Voice transcription uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework, subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

EasyGains is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and secure authentication. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
